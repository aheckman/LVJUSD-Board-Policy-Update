# 3523 - Electronic Signatures

**Type:** BP  
**Version:** Proposed  
**Status (from API):** DRAFT - KEEP CSBA  
**Original Adopted:** 06/01/2022  
**Last Revised:** Pending

---

## Policy Content

The Board of Education believes that the use of electronic records and signatures is a convenient paperless option that can increase efficiency in commercial and administrative transactions, reduce costs, and contribute to environmental sustainability in District operations. The Board authorizes the use of electronic signatures in District operations when authorized by law. 

The Superintendent or designee shall ensure that any electronic signature utilized by the District conforms with criteria described in law and that the level of security is sufficient for the transaction being conducted.  (Government Code 16.5; 2 CCR 22003, 22005)

The Superintendent or designee shall retain electronic records in accordance with law and regulations, and as specified in BP/AR 3580 - District Records.

# 9122 - Secretary

**Type:** BB  
**Version:** Proposed  
**Status (from API):** DRAFT - USE DISTRICT  
**Original Adopted:** 07/01/1984  
**Last Revised:** Pending

## Policy Content

The Board of Education shall appoint the Superintendent to serve as secretary to the Board. The Secretary to the Board shall be responsible for maintaining an accurate and complete record of all Board proceedings and shall:


	Prepare, distribute and maintain the Board agenda
	 
	Record, distribute and maintain the Board minutes
	 
	Maintain Board records and documents
	 
	Conduct official correspondence and communication for the Board
	 
	As directed by the Board, sign and execute official papers
	 
	Perform other duties as assigned by the Board


The secretary shall also be responsible for the following functions:


	Preparing for Board meetings:
	 
	
		Consult with Board President and develop the agenda.
		 
		Maintain a calendar of the Board's unfinished business and remind the Board of its own decision concerning scheduling these items.
		 
		Call to the attention of the Board legal provisions, forms, and requirements in those matters for which the secretary is responsible.
		 
		Arrange for professional consultants to attend meetings as needed.
		 
		Draft policy motions and resolutions upon the Board's request and with its advice.
		 
	
	
	Attending all Board meetings and assuring physical arrangements are in order:
	 
	
		The flag of the United States of America shall be properly displayed in the Board room during all meetings of the Board.
		 
		Adequate supplies and materials are available for the Board to conduct its business.
		 
		Audio/visual and recording equipment are available as needed.
		 
		Seating for the audience, including the press, has been arranged and copies of the agenda and other materials pertinent to the meeting are available.
		 
	
	
	Record Board decisions and the deposition of all matters in which the Board considered action.
	 
	Advise the Board of pertinent provisions of the California Education or Government Codes concerning its duties.
	 
	Maintain Board's reference files and reference materials.
	
	Alert Board to miscellaneous information sent which may be pertinent to Board affairs.
	Handle correspondence:
	 
	
		Log and forward correspondence received for the Board.
		 
		Seek instruction for replies when needed.
		 
		Draft replies in advance, when possible, for Board consideration.
		 
		Acknowledge all gifts and donations to the District with thank you letters to the donors.
		 
	
	
	Supervise the District's publicity.
	
	All District publicity shall be under the general authority of the Superintendent with the Board serving in an advisory capacity.
	 
	Arrange for District elections.
	​​​​​​​
	
		Assure all election requirements and deadlines are met.
		 
		Provide for orientation of new Board members, which includes arranging a meeting with the Human Resources Department to discuss stipend and benefits prior to swearing into office.
